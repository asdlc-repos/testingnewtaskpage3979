# Overview

An internal leave management system where WSO2 employees can request time off and managers can review and approve those requests.

# Personas

- Employee — requests leave and checks their balance
- Manager — approves or rejects leave requests from their team

# Features

- An employee submits a leave request with dates and reason.
- Managers see a list of pending requests from people they manage.
- A manager can approve or reject each request.
- Employees see the status of their submitted requests.
- Everyone can view their current leave balance and entitlement.
- employees should be type and add a name as a lead
